Me!

Well, what can I say about me? I've been working as The Seasonal Stitcher since May, 2017 on Etsy. I love, love, love crafting, especially sewing and knitting (if you haven't already noticed!) I like to make items that are one-of-a-kind, unpredictable, fun and feminine all at the same time! I have been crafting since I was 7 years old - I'm now a gal in my mid-thirties! Saying that, the passion and motivation has only got stronger throughout the years. I'm not complete without a sewing needle or a pair of knitting needles in my hands. The inspiration for The Seasonal Stitcher came to me in a dream; I literally dreamt about owning a store where I made all my goods by hand for all the seasons throughout the year. And that's it, really, in a nutshell. So, dreams really do come true.
